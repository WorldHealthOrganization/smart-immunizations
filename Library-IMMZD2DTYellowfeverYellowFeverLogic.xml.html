 * Library: IMMZD2DTYellowfeverYellowFeverLogic (IMMZ.D2.DT.Yellow fever)

 * Trigger: IMMZ.D2 Determine required vaccination(s) in any
 */

    and Encounter.&quot;No live vaccine was administered in the past 4 weeks&quot;
