StructureDefinition-IMMZ_D1_Capture_or_update_client_history

Description: Data elements for the IMMZ.D1.Capture or update client history Data Dictionary Activity.

Type: object

FHIR Page: StructureDefinition-IMMZ_D1_Capture_or_update_client_history.html

Properties:

IMMZD1 (array)
Data elements for the IMMZ.D1.Capture or update client history Data Dictionary Activity.
patient (object)
The patient.
birthDose (boolean)
Indicates if the client received a dose within 24 hours of birth. Whether a birth dose is counted as part of the primary series will depend on the antigen
typeOfDose (object)
The type of dose in a series that the client received
completedThePrimaryVaccinationSeries (boolean)
Indicates if the client has completed the primary vaccination series of a product/antigen. If the client has not yet completed their primary series, it means they may be expected to receive more doses to complete their vaccination regimen for the respective product/antigen
completedTheBoosterSeries (boolean)
Indicates if the client has completed the booster series of a product/antigen
dateWhenPrimaryVaccinationSeriesWasCompleted (string)
The date when the client completed the primary vaccination series (per product/antigen)
hivStatus (object)
The current HIV status of the client
pretermBirth (boolean)
The infant was preterm; the mother gave birth to the infant when gestational age was less than 37 weeks
immunocompromised (boolean)
The client is known to be immunocompromised. This means the client has a weakened immune system and having a reduced ability to fight infections and other diseases
currentlyOnArt (boolean)
The client is currently receiving antiretroviral therapy (ART)
typeOfTbInfectionTestPerformed (object)
The type of tuberculosis (TB) infection test performed
tbInfectionTestResult (object)
Records the result of the TB infection test
immunologicallyStable (boolean)
The client is living with HIV and is immunologically stable with CD4 count or percentage above a minimum as defined in the HIV guidelines
clinicallyWell (boolean)
The client is living with HIV and is clinically well, as defined in HIV guidelines and WHO clinical staging
birthWeightInGrams (number)
Represents the client's birth weight value measures in grams
typeOfPoliovirusDose (object)
The type of the poliovirus vaccine dose administered to the client
artStartDate (string)
The date on which the client started or restarted ART
highRiskOfPneumococcalInfection (boolean)
The client is at high risk for pneumococcal infection because of underlying medical conditions (i.e. HIV infection or sickle-cell disease)
typeOfJeDose (object)
The type of Japanese encephalitis (JE) vaccine dose administered to the client
typeOfTbeDose (object)
The type of tick-borne encephalitis (TBE) vaccine dose administered
typeOfTyphoidDose (object)
The type of typhoid vaccine dose administered to the client
typeOfCholeraDose (object)
The type of cholera vaccine dose administered to the client
typeOfMeningococcalDose (object)
The type of meningococcal vaccine dose administered to the client
ageInMonthsWhenClientReceivedFirstMeningococcalDose (number)
The age in months when client received their first meningococcal dose
typeOfHepatitisADose (object)
The type of hepatitis A-containing vaccine dose administered to the client
typeOfHepatitisBDose (object)
The type of hepatitis B-containing vaccine dose administered to the client
vnaLevel (number)
Vaccine-induced neutralizing antibody (VNA) level, measured as a serum antibody concentration from the result of the concentrated, purified cell culture-derived and embryonated egg-based rabies vaccines (CCEEV)
riskOfOccupationalExposureToRabiesVirus (boolean)
Professional at risk of occupational exposure to rabies virus, particularly animal health workers. Also considered for medical professionals who regularly provide care to persons with rabies
dengueSerostatus (object)
Indicates the result of a serological test determining whether the client has antibodies against the dengue virus
vaccineType (array)
Vaccine type/category that was administered or was to be administered. Any vaccine code available in the IMMZ.Z Vaccine library list of codes applies in this data element
dateAndTimeOfVaccination (string)
Represents the visit/encounter date, which is the date and time when each vaccine was administered to the client
contactDate (string)
The date and time of the client's contact
ancContactNumber (integer)
The antenatal care (ANC) contact or visit number
doseNumber (integer)
Vaccine dose number including dose number within series

Required fields: patient, typeOfDose, vaccineType, dateAndTimeOfVaccination, doseNumber
