ValueSet: IMMZD2DTTBEEnceVir ValueSet for Decision Table

Official URL: http://smart.who.int/immunizations/ValueSet/IMMZD2DTTBEEnceVirVS Version: 0.2.0
Active as of 2025-11-25 Computable Name: IMMZD2DTTBEEnceVirVS

ValueSet IMMZD2DTTBEEnceVir for IMMZ.D2.DT.TBE.EnceVir. Business rule: Determine if the client is due for a tick-borne encephalitis (TBE) vaccination according to the national immunization schedule Trigger: IMMZ.D2 Determine required vaccination(s) if any Table: EnceVir schedule (In areas where the disease is highly endemic, implying that there is a high individual risk of infection, WHO recommends that vaccination be offered to all age groups, including children )
