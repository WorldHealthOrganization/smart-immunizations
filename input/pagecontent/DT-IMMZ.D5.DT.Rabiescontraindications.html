Individuals with a history of severe hypersensitivity to any of the components or to excipients listed by the vaccine manufacturer should receive an alternative rabies vaccine product for pre-exposure prophylaxis (PreP). In view of the almost invariably fatal outcome of rabies, there is no contraindication to PrEP vaccination. WHO recommendations apply only to inactivated modern, concentrated, purified cell culture and embryonated egg-based rabies vaccines (CCEEVs).
