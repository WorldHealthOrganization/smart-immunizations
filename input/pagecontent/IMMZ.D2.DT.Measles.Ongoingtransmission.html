Measles primary series is complete. Two measles primary series doses were administered. An additional dose of MCV should be administered to children infected with HIV receiving highly active antiretroviral therapy (HAART) following immune reconstitution. If CD4+ T lymphocyte counts are monitored, an additional dose of MCV should be administered when immune reconstitution has been achieved (e.g. when the CD4+ T lymphocyte count reaches 20–25%). Where CD4+ T lymphocyte monitoring is not available, children should receive an additional dose of MCV 6–12 months after initiation of HAART.
