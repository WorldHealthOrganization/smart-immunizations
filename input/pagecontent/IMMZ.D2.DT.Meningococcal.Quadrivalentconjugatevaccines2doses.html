Quadrivalent conjugate vaccines (A,C,W135,Y-D and A,C,W135,Y-CRM) should be administered as one single intramuscular dose to individuals aged ≥ 2 years. A,C,W135,Y-D is also licensed for children aged 9–23 months and given as a 2-dose series 3 months apart, beginning at 9 months of age.
