CYD-TDV is not recommended in pregnant and lactating women because insufficient data are available on its use in pregnancy. However, the limited data generated from inadvertent vaccination of pregnant women that occurred during clinical trials have not identified a specific risk. If a woman becomes pregnant before all 3 doses have been administered, the remaining doses should be given after lactation has been concluded.
